Questions? We're here to help.

How does the 2-month free trial work?

No payment information is needed to sign up for ZenPayroll. Your first two months are free with unlimited payroll runs, including correction and bonus payrolls.Once your first two months have passed, we will automatically debit your company bank account each month so there's no need for invoicing. After your free trial, pricing starts at just $29/month.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

There’s no contract – just pay as you go, month-to-month. You can cancel at any time without penalty.

What if I need to add more employees?

Adding employees or contractors at any time is incredibly easy. Plus, we automatically file new hire reports and generate 1099's for you. We will automatically update your monthly price based on the number of people paid that month.

Do I need to download an app to run payroll?

No, you don't need to download anything to use ZenPayroll. You can access payroll online at any time from any web-enabled device. This includes computers, tablets and smartphones. Easily run payroll while you're on vacation!

How do I pay after my free trial?

After your free trial is up, we will automatically debit your company bank account each month so you never have to worry about invoicing.
