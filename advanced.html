Payroll – perfectly tailored

No matter how you choose to pay your employees, we’ve got you covered. Run payroll as many times as you need, at your convenience. Best of all, everything is included in your one monthly price.

  • Unlimited bonus & off-cycle payrolls

    You can reward your employees at any time. No need to wait until the next pay period.

  • Cancel payroll

    Made a mistake? No worries – quickly cancel a payroll with a single click and re-run it when you’re ready.

  • Flexible payment schedules

    Choose how often employees get to celebrate payday: weekly, bi-weekly, twice a month, or monthly.

Net-to-Gross

  • No calculator needed

    You should be able to give bonuses on the spot without having to make tax calculations. Simply enter the net amount your employees should receive, and we’ll figure out the gross amount you need to pay before taxes.

Detailed reports at your fingertips

Get a better understanding of your payroll. Review detailed cost reports, payroll journals, and bank transaction statements.

Report Types:

  • Payroll Journal / History

  • Benefits Report

  • Year-to-Date Report

  • Employee Information

  • Contractor Information

  • Contractor Payments

  • Bank Account Transactions

  • Paid Time Off Summary

  • Tax Payments Report
