Taxes – paid and filed

Never worry about calculating, filing or paying your taxes. We take care of everything so you can spend time on what’s important to you and your employees.

  • Tax calculations & payments

    ZenPayroll automatically calculates your state and federal payroll taxes. We then electronically pay them to the government on your behalf.

  • Automatic tax filings

    No more paper forms. Beginning with your first payroll run, ZenPayroll creates and electronically files your State & Federal payroll tax documents, including all end-of-year forms such as the W-2. Access your forms online at any time.

Set it and forget it

Payroll on AutoPilot™

Never worry about missing a deadline. With Payroll on AutoPilot™ activated, our system will automatically process your company's payroll and let you know you’re good to go. Your employees will be paid on time, every time.

With all the time saved, feel free to:

  • Grab tea with a customer
  • Tend to your bonsai garden
  • Go for a walk with your team

Easily pay your contractors

We give you the flexibility to work with independent contractors when you need them.

  • 1099 Forms

    There’s no extra cost to generate 1099 forms at the end of the year. We will automatically generate and send them electronically to your contractors, plus file them with your tax documents.
